Work with us

We are always looking for great team members! If you are interested in the work we do in the lab, please get in touch.

Research assistants and Independent study

We are also always working with CMU undergraduate and masters students on research projects. For an overview of research directions, please check out our opportunities page. Check back regularly as this document is updated frequently.

To join a project, please email the contact person as listed in the project description and include the following information:
  • your CV
  • projects shown on a website or PDF portfolio
  • your latest grade transcript
  • your preferred starting date.
For general questions, you can email Prof. Alexandra Ion. To join a specific project, please contact the corresponding project lead.

PhD students and Internships

If you are interested in a PhD position with us, please make sure to apply to HCII. Find all information and deadlines on the admission site.

We are also accepting applications for internship positions. To apply, please send an email to Prof. Alexandra Ion. and include:
  • Your CV.
  • Work samples: a website providing an overview of your work. For group projects, please clearify what your contributions are.
  • Your latest grade transcript.
  • Your preferred starting date and internship duration.
  • E-Mail addresses of two recommendation letter writers.
